Find health care professionals

See a GP if you want help with menopause symptoms.

If your GP cannot help, there are menopause specialists who have experience in supporting anyone going through perimenopause and menopause.

  • Find your nearest menopause specialist (this lists private and NHS specialists) Psychological therapies like counselling or CBT (cognitive behavioural therapy) can help with symptoms of perimenopause and menopause. You can get NHS psychological therapies without seeing a GP first.
  • Find an NHS psychological therapies service
